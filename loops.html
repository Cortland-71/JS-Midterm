Okay so in this section we're going to take a look at two kinds of loops in Java. The while loop and the for loop. Let's first take a look at the while loop.


The while loop is the simplest form of a loop in Java. Much like the if statment in the previous section, it only executes if a condition is true. The big difference from the if statment however is that it will continue to execute over and over again until the condition is false. Let's look at an example.

More advanced techniques might include nested loops. We won't be going into this here but know that you can nest loops inside of other loops.
